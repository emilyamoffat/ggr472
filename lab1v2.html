Bruce's Best Ice Cream

#1

Lloyds Shop and Pool Hall is my number one pick! Located in Wiarton, from the outside this pool hall and smoke shop doesn't look like much, but inside, they sell scoops and pints of the best small-batch ice cream around. Stop in for a scoop of a unique flavour and take a walk to see Wiarton Willie!

Map

Big Bay Ice Cream

#2

Big Bay General Store is worth the stop for a scoop of ice cream and a picturesque view. The ice cream is fresh churned onsite and never disappoints. Bring your bathing suit to jump of the pier!

Map

Sauble Beach House and Cafe

#3

Sauble Beach House is the place to be at sunset on any summer's evening. Serving Kawartha Dairy Ice cream, this spot makes the list because the location is so perfect. Sauble beach is known for beautiful sunsets that paint the sky incredble colours, pick up a cone and watch the natural show.
